    Kunskapsskolan Educational Program (KED) was conceptualized in 1999 in Sweden.

     

    KED program focuses on personalized education, individualized goals and creates students who take responsibility and ownership for their learning. This lays the foundation for a lifetime of learning as students develop the confidence that comes from setting high academic goals and consistently planning how to meet them, and ultimately achieving most.
